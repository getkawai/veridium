## 💼 Hold-to-Earn (Revenue Share)

**Earn USDT just by holding KAWAI tokens!**

### How It Works

**After mining phase ends:**

1. All platform revenue (USDT) collected weekly
2. Revenue distributed to KAWAI holders
3. Your share = Your tokens / Total supply
4. Weekly distributions via Merkle tree

**Example:**
```
Weekly revenue: 10,000 USDT
Your KAWAI: 100,000 (0.01% of 1B supply)
Your share: 10,000 × 0.01% = 1 USDT per week
Yearly: 52 USDT from just holding!
```

### Benefits

- ✅ **Passive income** - No action required

- ✅ **No lock required** - Just hold in your wallet

**Phase 2: Hold-to-Earn (After 1B supply)**

- All revenue distributed to holders
- KAWAI becomes dividend-paying asset

## 🔄 Reward Lifecycle

### 1. Accumulation (Off-Chain)

All rewards accumulate in Cloudflare KV storage:
- **Instant tracking** - Recorded immediately
- **Gas-free** - No blockchain fees
- **Detailed records** - Full history maintained

### 2. Settlement (Weekly)

Every Monday at 00:00 UTC:
- Admin collects all pending rewards
- Generates Merkle tree (cryptographic proof)
- Uploads Merkle root to blockchain
- Individual proofs stored off-chain

### 3. Claiming (On-Chain)

Anytime after settlement:
- Go to Rewards dashboard
- Click "Claim" on any reward
- Provide Merkle proof
- Smart contract verifies and mints tokens
- You pay gas fee (~0.001 MON)

### 4. Usage

Use your earned KAWAI:
- Hold for dividends (Phase 2)
- Trade on marketplace
- Future: Pay for services with KAWAI

[Learn how to claim →](claiming.md)

## 🆘 Common Questions

**Q: Can I earn multiple reward types at once?**  
A: Yes! All reward systems work together. You can earn use-to-earn, deposit cashback, referral commissions, and mining rewards simultaneously.

**Q: When can I claim my rewards?**  
A: After weekly settlement (Monday 00:00 UTC). Check your Rewards dashboard for claimable amounts.

**Q: Do rewards expire?**  
A: No! Your accumulated rewards never expire. Claim whenever convenient.

**Q: Is there a minimum to claim?**  
A: No minimum for claiming. However, consider gas fees (~0.001 MON) when claiming small amounts.

**Q: Can I transfer my rewards to someone else?**  
A: Yes, after claiming, KAWAI tokens are yours to transfer, trade, or hold.
