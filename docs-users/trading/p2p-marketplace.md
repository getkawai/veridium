# P2P Marketplace

Trade KAWAI tokens directly with other users in a decentralized marketplace.

## 🎯 What is P2P Trading?

The P2P (peer-to-peer) marketplace allows you to:
- **Buy KAWAI** with USDT from other users
- **Sell KAWAI** for USDT to other users
- **Set your own prices** - no forced rates
- **Trade securely** - smart contract escrow

**No middleman, no liquidity pool, no slippage!**

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites

Before trading, you need:
- ✅ Kawai desktop app installed
- ✅ Wallet set up and funded
- ✅ Small amount of MON for gas fees
- ✅ USDT (for buying) or KAWAI (for selling)

### Access the Marketplace

1. Open Kawai app
2. Click **Trading** in sidebar
3. Select **P2P Marketplace**
4. Browse available orders

## 💰 Buying KAWAI

### Step-by-Step Guide

**1. Browse Sell Orders**
- View all available sell orders
- Sort by price (lowest first)
- Check seller reputation
- See order details (amount, price, total)

**2. Select an Order**
- Click on order you want to fill
- Review details carefully:
  - KAWAI amount
  - Price per KAWAI
  - Total USDT cost
  - Seller address

**3. Confirm Purchase**
- Enter amount to buy (partial fills allowed)
- Review total cost
- Click **Buy KAWAI**
- Approve USDT spending (first time only)
- Confirm transaction

**4. Receive Tokens**
- USDT transferred to seller
- KAWAI transferred to you
- Transaction complete!
- Check your wallet balance

### Tips for Buyers

✅ **Compare prices** - Check multiple orders  
✅ **Start small** - Test with small amounts first  
✅ **Check reputation** - Look for established sellers  
✅ **Calculate fees** - Factor in gas costs  
✅ **Be patient** - Better prices may appear

## 💎 Selling KAWAI

### Step-by-Step Guide

**1. Create Sell Order**
- Click **Create Order** button
- Select **Sell KAWAI**
- Enter details:
  - Amount of KAWAI to sell
  - Price per KAWAI (in USDT)
  - Total USDT you'll receive

**2. Set Your Price**
- Check current market prices
- Consider:
  - Recent trades
  - Competing orders
  - Your urgency to sell
- Set competitive price

**3. Approve & Create**
- Approve KAWAI spending (first time)
- Confirm order creation
- Pay gas fee (~0.001 MON)
- Order goes live immediately

**4. Wait for Buyer**
- Your KAWAI is locked in escrow
- Order visible to all buyers
- Automatic execution when filled
- USDT sent to your wallet

### Tips for Sellers

✅ **Price competitively** - Check existing orders  
✅ **Be realistic** - Too high = no buyers  
✅ **Partial fills** - Allow buyers to take portions  
✅ **Update orders** - Cancel and recreate if needed  
✅ **Track market** - Adjust prices as needed

## 📊 Order Management

### View Your Orders

**Active Orders:**
- Orders waiting to be filled
- Can be cancelled anytime
- Tokens locked in escrow

**Order History:**
- Completed trades
- Cancelled orders
- Transaction details

### Cancel Orders

**To cancel an order:**
1. Go to **My Orders** tab
2. Find order to cancel
3. Click **Cancel Order**
4. Confirm transaction
5. Tokens returned to wallet

**Note:** You pay gas fee to cancel

### Partial Fills

Orders can be filled partially:
- Buyer takes 50% → Order remains for 50%
- Multiple buyers can fill one order
- Seller receives USDT for each fill
- Order closes when fully filled

## 💡 Pricing Strategy

**For Buyers:**

### Price Factors

**Increases price:**
- High platform usage
- Strong revenue sharing

- Positive news

**Decreases price:**

- Competition

## 🔒 Security Features

### Smart Contract Escrow

**How it works:**
1. Seller creates order → KAWAI locked
2. Buyer fills order → USDT sent
3. Smart contract executes swap
4. Both parties receive tokens

**Benefits:**
- No trust needed
- Atomic swaps (all or nothing)
- No counterparty risk
- Transparent and verifiable

### Safety Tips

✅ **Verify addresses** - Double-check before confirming  
✅ **Start small** - Test with small amounts  
✅ **Check gas fees** - Ensure you have enough MON  
✅ **Review carefully** - Read all details before confirming  
❌ **Never share** - Private keys or seed phrases

## 💸 Fees & Costs

### Trading Fees

**Platform fees:**
- **0% trading fee** - No platform commission
- Only pay blockchain gas fees

**Gas fees (approximate):**
- Create order: ~0.001 MON
- Fill order: ~0.002 MON
- Cancel order: ~0.001 MON

### Cost Calculation

**Example Buy:**
```
KAWAI amount: 1,000
Price: 0.50 USDT/KAWAI
USDT cost: 500 USDT
Gas fee: 0.002 MON (~$0.01)
Total: 500 USDT + gas
```

**Example Sell:**
```
KAWAI amount: 1,000
Price: 0.50 USDT/KAWAI
USDT received: 500 USDT
Gas fee: 0.001 MON (~$0.005)
Net: 500 USDT - gas
```

## 🆘 Common Issues

### "Insufficient Balance"
- Check USDT balance (for buying)
- Check KAWAI balance (for selling)
- Ensure enough MON for gas

### "Transaction Failed"
- Increase gas limit
- Check network status
- Verify token approvals
- Try again later

### "Order Not Filling"
- Price may be uncompetitive
- Cancel and create new order
- Adjust price closer to market

### "Can't Cancel Order"
- Ensure enough MON for gas
- Wait for pending transactions
- Check network congestion
