# General FAQ

## What is Kawai DeAI Network?

Kawai DeAI Network is a decentralized AI compute platform built on Monad blockchain. We provide affordable AI services (chat and image generation) while rewarding users and contributors with KAWAI tokens.

## Is Kawai DeAI free to use?

New users get a **5 USDT free trial** (10 USDT with referral code) to try our AI services. After that, you pay with USDT but earn 5% cashback in KAWAI tokens on every request.

## What blockchain does Kawai use?

We're built on **Monad blockchain** - a high-performance EVM-compatible chain with fast transactions and low fees.

## Is my wallet secure?

Yes! Your wallet is:
- **Self-custodial** - Only you control your private keys
- **Built-in** - No need for external wallet apps
- **Encrypted** - Private keys stored securely on your device
- **Recoverable** - Backup your seed phrase to restore anytime

## How do I get KAWAI tokens?

You can earn KAWAI tokens through:
1. **Use-to-Earn**: 5% cashback on AI requests
2. **Deposit Cashback**: 1-5% on USDT deposits
3. **Referral Rewards**: Invite friends and earn commissions
4. **Mining**: Contribute GPU power (coming soon)
5. **P2P Trading**: Buy from other users

## When can I claim my rewards?

Rewards are settled **weekly every Monday at 00:00 UTC**. After settlement, you can claim anytime from your Rewards dashboard.

## Do I need MON tokens?

Yes, you need a small amount of MON (Monad's native token) for:
- Claiming rewards (~0.001 MON per claim)
- Trading on P2P marketplace
- Other blockchain transactions

The app will notify you when you need MON.

## Can I use Kawai on mobile?

Currently, Kawai is available as a **desktop application** (Windows, macOS, Linux). Mobile app is planned for future release.

## Is there a minimum deposit?

No minimum! You can deposit any amount of USDT. However, larger deposits unlock higher cashback tiers (1-5%).

## How do I get support?

- **In-app**: Click Help → Contact Support
- **Discord**: Join our community server
- **Email**: support@kawai.network
- **Docs**: Browse this documentation

## Is Kawai open source?

Yes! Our smart contracts and core infrastructure are open source on GitHub. You can verify all code and contribute to the project.
