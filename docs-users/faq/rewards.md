# Rewards FAQ

## How does Use-to-Earn work?

Every AI request you make earns you **5% cashback in KAWAI tokens**. For example:
- Request costs 0.10 USDT
- You earn ~2.5 KAWAI tokens back
- Rewards accumulate automatically

## What is the deposit cashback tier system?

Your cashback rate increases with total deposits:

| Total Deposits | Tier | Cashback Rate |
|----------------|------|---------------|
| < 100 USDT | Bronze | 1% |
| 100-500 USDT | Silver | 2% |
| 500-1K USDT | Gold | 3% |
| 1K-5K USDT | Platinum | 4% |
| ≥ 5K USDT | Diamond | 5% |

**First deposit always gets 5%** regardless of amount!

## How do referral rewards work?

When you invite a friend:
1. **You earn**: 5 USDT one-time bonus
2. **Friend earns**: 10 USDT signup bonus
3. **You earn**: 5% of their mining rewards forever

## When are rewards settled?

**Every Monday at 00:00 UTC**, we:
1. Calculate all pending rewards
2. Generate Merkle proofs
3. Upload to blockchain
4. Make rewards claimable

## Can I claim multiple weeks at once?

Yes! You can claim rewards from multiple settlement periods in a single transaction to save on gas fees.

## Why do I need to pay gas to claim?

Claiming mints new KAWAI tokens on the blockchain, which requires a small transaction fee (~0.001 MON). This ensures security and decentralization.

## Do rewards expire?

No! Your accumulated rewards never expire. Claim whenever convenient.

## What is Hold-to-Earn?

After the mining phase ends (1B KAWAI minted), all platform revenue (USDT) will be distributed weekly to KAWAI holders proportional to their holdings.

## How is mining different from use-to-earn?

- **Use-to-Earn**: Earn by using AI services (5% cashback)
- **Mining**: Earn by contributing GPU power (85-90% of generated tokens)

Both can be done simultaneously!

## What happens if I don't claim my rewards?

Nothing bad! Rewards stay in your account indefinitely. However, claiming regularly helps you:
- Track your earnings
- Use tokens for trading
- Participate in Hold-to-Earn (future)

## Can I transfer unclaimed rewards?

No. Rewards must be claimed first (minted as tokens) before you can transfer or trade them.

## Is there a cap on earnings?

- **Use-to-Earn**: No cap
- **Deposit Cashback**: 20,000 KAWAI per deposit (Diamond tier)
- **Referral**: No cap
- **Mining**: Limited by total supply (1B KAWAI)
