### 🔒 Privacy-First Architecture
**"Your Conversations Stay on Your Device"**

**How Kawai Works:**
```
Your Desktop App (Local)          Contributor Network (Distributed)
─────────────────────────         ────────────────────────────────
✅ Chat History Stored            ✅ AI Model Inference
✅ Conversation Context           ✅ Decentralized Processing
✅ Your Private Keys              ✅ Earn Rewards for Computing
✅ Wallet & Balances              ✅ No Data Retention
✅ Settings & Preferences         
                                  
Your data NEVER leaves            Contributors ONLY process
your device permanently           requests (no storage)
```

**What Makes Kawai Different:**
- ✅ **Local Chat History** - All conversations stored on your device only
- ✅ **Distributed AI Processing** - Contributors process requests but don't store data
- ✅ **No Central Database** - No company server storing your conversations
- ✅ **Zero Tracking** - No analytics, no telemetry, no surveillance
- ✅ **End-to-End Encryption** - Requests encrypted between you and contributors
- ✅ **Self-Custody Wallet** - You control your private keys, always

**Traditional AI vs Kawai:**
```
ChatGPT/Claude                   Kawai
──────────────                   ─────────────
Your Browser                     Your Desktop App
    ↓                                ↓
Their Cloud ❌                   Local Storage ✅
    ↓                                ↓
Their Database ❌                Contributor Network
    ↓                            (No Data Retention) ✅
Permanent Storage ❌                 ↓
    ↓                            Back to Your Device ✅
Training Data ❌
```

**Key Privacy Features:**
- 🔒 **Conversations** - Stored locally, never uploaded to central servers
- 🔒 **API Keys** - Encrypted locally, never transmitted
- 🔒 **Wallet Keys** - Generated and stored on your device only
- 🔒 **Usage Patterns** - Not tracked or analyzed
- 🔒 **Personal Data** - Never collected or shared
